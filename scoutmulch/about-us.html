About Troop 27B and G

Troop 27 B and G operates out of Plano, TX. Our troop participates in many scouting activities during the year. Our fundraisers pay for those activities, especially our summer camps! Our younger scouts go to Camp Hale in Oklahoma, where they work on merit badges they need for rank advancement and learn fun things like sailing, robotics, or welding. Our older scouts go on high adventure where they get to summit mountains, backpack across country, raft river rapids, zipline across canyons, and learn to ride ATVs.

Summer Camps

Camp Hale

Camp Hale is near Taihina, Oklahoma and is adjacent to the Ouachita National Forest. They have a beautiful lake where many of our scouts choose to get up early and participate in the mile swim. We love their Soaring to Eagle program where our first year campers complete many of the requirements to advance to First Class. Their Horsemanship merit badge culminates in a Scout/Parent trail ride up the mountain. They offer every kind of merit badge, from Eagle required to technology to winter sports on their simulated ski slope!
